Apache Kafka is a widely used distributed streaming platform, and it historically relied on Apache Zookeeper for cluster management and coordination. However, Kafka has been evolving, and one of the significant recent developments is the introduction of "KRaft" (Kafka Raft), which is designed to eliminate the need for Zookeeper in managing Kafka clusters. Let's delve into the differences and implications of using Zookeeper vs. KRaft in Kafka.
